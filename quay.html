Brayford Quay

Waterside accommodation in Lincoln opposite the University

About Brayford Quay

Brayford Quay is a University Accredited, superbly located student accommodation with outstanding facilities for you to enjoy. Apartments of 5 and 6 bed en-suite rooms share a kitchen / communal area and we also offer a range of self-contained studios. All rooms are en-suite with some having fantastic views of the marina.Enjoy time in our gym, common room with TV, pool table and games console or walk along the marina enjoying the many restaurants and bars with spectacular views. Rents are all inclusive of utility bills, WIFI internet and gym membership.

About the Area

Brayford Quay is situated just moments from the Quay and it's wonderful bars, restaurant and pubs. Being two minutes from the main high street containing all the entertainment, shopping and paths to the historical cathedral and castle. Nearby in town there is the local market, with fresh meat and vegetable available and the shopping center across the bridge.
